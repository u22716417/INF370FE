Help-Services list

This page allows you to manage services within the system. Here are the available actions:

  • Add New Service: Click on the "Add New" button to add a new service. This will redirect you to the form where you can enter the details for a new service.
  • View Services List: The table below displays all the services currently in the system, including their name and description.
  • Edit Service: To edit a service, click on the "Edit" button next to the service you want to modify. This will open a form pre-filled with the services's current information.
  • Delete Service: To delete a service, click on the "Delete" button next to the corresponding service. A confirmation dialog may appear to ensure you want to proceed with the deletion. Note that this action is irreversible.
