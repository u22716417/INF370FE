Help-Sponsor list

This page allows you to manage sponsors within the system. Here are the available actions:

  • Add New Sponsor: Click on the "Add New" button to add a new sponsor. This will redirect you to the form where you can enter the details for a new sponsor.
  • Export to JSON: Click on the "Export to JSON" button to export the sponsor details to a JSON file.The file will be downloaded and saved to your local device.
  • Edit Sponsor: To edit a specific sponsor's details, click on the "Edit" button next to the sponsor you want to modify. This will open a form pre-filled with the sponsor's current information.
  • Delete Sponsor: To delete a sponsor, click on the "Delete" button next to the corresponding sponsor. A confirmation dialog may appear to ensure you want to proceed with the deletion. Note that this action is irreversible.
