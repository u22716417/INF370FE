Help-FAQ list

This page allows you to manage FAQ's within the system. Here are the available actions:

  • The table below displays all the FAQ currently in the system, including the question and answer.
  • Answer Question: Click on the "Answer Question" button to add an answer to a question. This will redirect you to the form where you can enter the details for the answer to the question.
  • Delete FAQ: To delete a FAQ, click on the "Delete" button next to the corresponding FAQ. A confirmation dialog may appear to ensure you want to proceed with the deletion. Note that this action is irreversible.
