Help-Equipment list

This page allows you to manage equipment within the system. Here are the available actions:

  • Add New Equipment: Click on the "Add New" button to add new equipment. This will redirect you to the form where you can enter the details for new equipment.
  • View Equipment List: The table below displays all the equipment currently in the system, including their name, description, availability, type, condition and image.
  • Edit Equipment: To edit equipment, click on the "Edit" button next to the equipment you want to modify. This will open a form pre-filled with the equipment's current information.
  • Delete Equipment: To delete equipment, click on the "Delete" button next to the corresponding equipment. A confirmation dialog may appear to ensure you want to proceed with the deletion. Note that this action is irreversible.
